This directory will contain sound effect files for the game:

- select.mp3: Sound when selecting a bulb
- swap.mp3: Sound when swapping bulbs
- match.mp3: Sound when bulbs are matched
- fall.mp3: Sound when bulbs are falling
- levelup.mp3: Sound when completing a level
- gameover.mp3: Sound when game is over

For development purposes, you can use free sound effects from sources like:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/
